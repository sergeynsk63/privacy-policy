Last Updated: November 11, 2025

Information Collection and Use

The Application collects limited, anonymous technical information to improve app functionality and user experience. We are committed to protecting your privacy and do not collect any personally identifiable information.

Data We Collect

The Application collects the following anonymous technical data:

  • Device Identifier (IDFV): A unique identifier assigned to apps from the same vendor on your device. This identifier is anonymous, cannot be used to track you across different apps or websites, and is automatically reset if you delete all apps from this vendor and reinstall them.
  • Platform Information: Operating system type (iOS)
  • OS Version: Your device's iOS version number
  • App Version: Version of Natrive Energy you're using
  • Language Setting: Your device's preferred language
  • Event Data: Anonymous usage events such as app launch and onboarding completion
  • Timestamp: Date and time of events

What We DO NOT Collect

  • No personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • No precise location data
  • No browsing history outside the app
  • No financial or payment information
  • No photos, contacts, or other personal files
  • No advertising identifier (IDFA)
  • No cross-app or cross-website tracking

Purpose of Data Collection

The anonymous technical data we collect is used solely for:

  • App Functionality: Ensuring the app works correctly on different devices and OS versions
  • Performance Monitoring: Identifying and fixing bugs and crashes
  • Feature Optimization: Understanding which features are used to improve the app experience
  • Analytics: Analyzing anonymous usage patterns to improve app design and functionality

Data Storage and Security

All collected data is:

  • Transmitted securely using HTTPS encryption
  • Stored on secure servers
  • Anonymous and cannot be linked to any individual user
  • Retained only as long as necessary for analytics purposes
  • Not sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes

Third-Party Services

The Application does not integrate any third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or tracking SDKs. All data is sent directly to our own analytics endpoint for internal use only.

Stock Market Data

The Application displays real-time stock market information obtained from Yahoo Finance public API. This data is used solely for educational and informational purposes. We do not collect or store any information about which stocks you view or interact with.

WebView Content

The Application may display web content in certain circumstances. When viewing web content:

  • Standard web technologies (cookies, web storage) may be used by the displayed website
  • The Service Provider does not control or access data collected by external websites
  • You are subject to the privacy policy of the external website being displayed
